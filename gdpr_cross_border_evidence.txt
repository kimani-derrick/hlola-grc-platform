GDPR Cross-Border Data Transfers Documentation

This document outlines our procedures for cross-border data transfers under GDPR Chapter V.

1. Adequacy Decisions - We rely on adequacy decisions for transfers to approved countries
2. Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) - We use EU Commission approved SCCs
3. Binding Corporate Rules - We have implemented BCRs for intra-group transfers
4. Certification Schemes - We participate in approved certification schemes
5. Derogations - We use specific derogations where applicable

All transfers are documented and comply with GDPR requirements.
