GDPR Lawful Basis Documentation

This document outlines the lawful basis for processing personal data under GDPR Article 6.

1. Consent - We have obtained explicit consent from data subjects
2. Contract - Processing is necessary for contract performance
3. Legal obligation - Processing is required by law
4. Vital interests - Processing protects vital interests
5. Public task - Processing is necessary for public interest
6. Legitimate interests - Processing is necessary for legitimate interests

All processing activities have been documented and justified according to GDPR requirements.
