GDPR Data Protection Officer (DPO) Documentation

This document outlines our Data Protection Officer appointment and responsibilities under GDPR Article 37-39.

1. DPO Appointment - We have appointed a qualified DPO as required by GDPR
2. DPO Contact Details - DPO contact information is publicly available
3. DPO Independence - DPO operates independently and reports to highest management
4. DPO Tasks - DPO monitors compliance, provides advice, and acts as contact point
5. DPO Resources - DPO has adequate resources and access to personal data
6. DPO Protection - DPO is protected from dismissal or penalty for performing tasks

Our DPO meets all GDPR requirements and qualifications.
